## Purpose

This document is the master index of all architectural, strategic, and operational decisions made during the LES project. It is the summary layer. Each decision listed here corresponds to a full ADR (Architecture Decision Record) file — `ADR-YYYY-MM-DD.md` — stored in `LES-Master/docs/decisions/` and published to GitBook under the Decision Log section.

**Reading this document:** Use it to find a decision quickly. For full context, rationale, alternatives considered, and consequences — open the corresponding ADR.
