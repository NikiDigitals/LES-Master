# ADR-000010 — NikiDigitals Brand Architecture

**Date:** May 2026
**Status:** Accepted
**Decider:** NikiDigitals
**Version:** GitHub — Technical Reference

---

## Context

A decision was required on the professional brand identity under which this portfolio and its associated projects would be published. The primary consideration was whether the brand should be narrowly scoped to LES specifically, or broadly scoped to cover a wider professional identity encompassing multiple future projects.

---

## Decision

NikiDigitals is established as a multi-project professional brand covering Finance, Systems, and Technology.

**Brand scope:**
- Finance — financial systems, transformation, ERP
- Systems — architecture, enterprise system design, personal operating systems
- Technology — cloud infrastructure, AI, development

**LES position within the brand:** LES is the flagship project. It is the primary proof of work and the centrepiece of the portfolio. Other projects are smaller and sit directly under the NikiDigitals account without the LES prefix.

**Naming decision:** The section previously referred to as "Blog" is renamed "Insights" across all platforms. The term blog carries an informal connotation inconsistent with the executive tone of the brand. Insights positions the content as professional analysis and academic reflection.

---

## Rationale

A brand scoped only to LES would be limiting. The career target — Finance Transformation Architect — requires demonstrated breadth across finance, systems thinking, and technology. A brand that signals all three is more credible and more flexible as the portfolio grows.

Naming the content section "Insights" rather than "Blog" is a deliberate positioning decision. The content published will include academic research summaries, architectural decision rationale, and professional reflections. These deserve a professional label.

---

## Consequences

- All professional output is published under the NikiDigitals identity
- Future projects added to the portfolio sit within the NikiDigitals brand framework
- The Insights section on the website and LinkedIn is the publication channel for professional and academic content
- LES documentation always identifies itself as part of NikiDigitals: Life Enterprise System
