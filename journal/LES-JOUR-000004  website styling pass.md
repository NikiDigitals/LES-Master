Today was a full website day. After the content was in place from yesterday, the focus shifted entirely to the styling pass — applying the NikiDigitals brand system across every page in a single dedicated session. Navy Night, Circuit Blue, Syne Bold, DM Sans. The site now looks like a professional identity rather than a WordPress default.

Most of the day was methodical work — page by page, section by section. The kind of work that does not feel dramatic but produces something real at the end of it. The exception was a gap appearing at the bottom of the contact page that resisted explanation for longer than expected. The fix was a padding adjustment. Small in the end. Time-consuming to find. That is frontend development — the unglamorous reality of building anything that has to look right on a screen.

By close of day the website was complete: six pages live, fully styled, contact form configured with email delivery confirmed, and cross-linking architecture in place across all four portfolio layers. On the documentation side, the decisions index was formalised as a standalone document, the ADR template was created, the documentation protocol was tightened, and the roadmap reached v3.7 incorporating the founding conviction narrative, two new foundational principles, the full NikiDigitals brand system, and a professional experience section documenting six concurrent roles since October 2025.

Phase 0 is close to complete. The foundation is no longer a plan. It is a functioning public presence.
